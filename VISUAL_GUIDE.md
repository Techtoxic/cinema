## ğŸ­ Animation Patterns

### On Scroll
- **Fade In + Slide Up**: Section headers, text blocks
- **Stagger**: Grid items (0.1s delay each)
- **Scale In**: Cards, buttons, icons

### On Hover
- **Scale**: Buttons (1.05x), Cards (translate up 8px)
- **Color Transition**: Links, icons
- **Shadow Growth**: Cards get larger shadows
- **Image Zoom**: Images scale 1.1x inside container

### On Click
- **Ripple Effect**: Buttons
- **Smooth Transition**: Page changes
